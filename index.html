NATIONAL EXTERNAL QUALITY ASSESSMENT SCHEME (NEQAS)

NEQAS evaluates the performance of participating laboratories by assessing the integrity of the entire testing from sample receipt to releasing of test results. This would allow comparison of the laboratory's testing to the performance of a peer group and/or the national reference laboratory.

The panels offered by the Research Institute for Tropical Medicine consist of a combination of positive and negative samples that are designed to mimic patient samples. Completed tests are then encoded online via Oneworld Accuracy System (OASYS), a cloud-based application developed by 1WA, and the data are then analyzed under ISO13528. Performance reports are provided to the participants to assess their results and compare their performance against their peer group.

Participation in an EQAS program is required under local regulatory policies for a laboratory to operate.

REMINDERS

Kindly wear your face mask, face shield upon entering the RITM premises. RITM management reserves the right to refuse entry of personnel who will fail to comply.

Contractors shall proceed at the Administrative Entrance Guard (In front of the Flagpoles) and present the hard copy of the approved access permit and negative RT-PCR Result/s 48 hours of all incoming contractor/s prior to the start of activity to the security guard. The Security Guard shall check all negative RT-PCR test and coordinate with the Administrative Office to validate the approved letter.

Contractors shall undergo a temperature check before entering the building/access site. Rechecking of temperature shall be done to the contractor with temperature above 37.5 after fifteen (15) minutes. contractor with a temperature above 37.5 after rechecking shall be denied of entry.

Contractors shall fully accomplish the Health Declaration and Contact Tracing Form and submit to the designated guard at the entrance of the building.

Contractors with the symptoms for COVID-19 (such as cough, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat, muscle pains and aches and diarrhea-like symptoms) shall be denied entry.

RITM VENDOR REGISTRATION ePORTAL

It is mandatory to be registered as a supplier to do business with the Institute. As a registered supplier you shall be contacted for new Request For Quotations (RFQs) if your registered capabilities match the requirements of the RFQ. In addition you shall get access to update your requirements and credentials using the portal.

When you have completed the registration process, RITM Procurement Department shall take the necessary steps to review your credentials. Only after this review has been completed and approved can your company be entered into our Source File.

To register to the RITM Source File, please click on the "GET STARTED" button and follow the instructions.

WARNING TO SUPPLIERS

Should unscrupulous organizations/individuals address RITM's suppliers, invoking the Agency's name, and solicit monetary contributions, to be paid to them (e.g. requesting payment for advertising to be made in a so-called “RITM-sponsored” brochure). Please be aware that RITM does not approach customers or suppliers with sponsoring or advertising requests. Should any supplier be approached by any individual, company or organization requesting money, sponsorship, or other favours on behalf of RITM, they should immediately report the fact to RITM's Public Assistance Complaints Unit (pacu@ritm.gov.ph).
